Was ist ein natürlicher Schwimmteich/Pool?

Prof. Dr. Wolfram Kircher, Hochschule Anhalt und Prof. Dr. Andreas Thon, Hochschule Geisenheim – neu überarbeitet 2025

  • An additional filter element was added as a stream bed, sealed with a PVC liner and running parallel to the regeneration zone.
  • Through an overflow the water reaches a stream, which meanders about 20 m down to a separate regeneration pond with lush vegetation.
  • A pump permanently draws water through the gravel bed into this pipe and then into a regeneration zone on the east side.
  • A second circulation system transports water from a skimmer under the western deck to the southern regeneration area.
  • Partitioning and purification methods The regeneration area was filled with poor loam and covered with gravel.
  • A PVC liner was fitted into the complete pool and led out to form an adjacent shallow regeneration zone.
  • The weeping willow at the east side of the regeneration pond shades the vegetation in the morning.
  • In the background the swimming area opens to the regeneration planting.
  • Helophytes used consist chiefly of Iris pseudacorus, Lysimachia thyr- siflora, Acorus calamus, Caltha palustris and Lythrum salicaria.
  • Porous tufa stones provide a surface for slippery biofilm layers to develop, so the pool wall has to be brushed regularly.
